The Best Movies According to Daniel

My top 3 movies of all-time:

The Matrix

This movies is ahead of its time. It's got great action, a fantastic storyline, and unreal SPFX.

Fight Club

This is an interesting psychological drama with high-level acting from Brad Pitt and Edward Norton.

Superbad

This movie takes me back to when I saw it in high-school and thought this was the funniest thing ever! Still holds up!
